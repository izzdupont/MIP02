About Us

At Midwest IT Provider, we are more than just a service provider, we are passionate tech professionals on a mission to transform the IT industry. With over 20+ years of combined experience across industries such as oil, retail, manufacturing, engineering, and technology, our team has honed the expertise needed to tackle any challenge.

We’ve seen a troubling shift in the industry, with companies outsourcing IT services overseas and abandoning the personal, white-glove support that businesses deserve. As a company built on fairness, transparency, and ethical practices, we believe this trend is a disservice to businesses and to the talented professionals trained here at home.

That’s why Midwest IT Provider was founded to restore the integrity of IT services. We are committed to providing superior support directly to our customers. No middlemen. No outsourcing. Just businesses working hand-in-hand with skilled experts who genuinely care about their success.
